# Project Overview — VasuDev Chemicals

## What Is This?
A single-page marketing website for **VasuDev Chemicals** — an ISO-certified industrial & specialty chemical manufacturer based in Gujarat, India. The site is designed to generate global export/import inquiries from international buyers.

## Who Is It For?
- **International importers & distributors** — especially in Gulf countries, USA, Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia
- **Oil & gas industries** looking for industrial chemicals
- **Lubricant manufacturers** requiring specialty chemicals
- **Waste water treatment companies** needing treatment chemicals
- **Any industry** already buying our chemicals under different brand names from other vendors

## What Problem Does It Solve?
VasuDev Chemo Pharma manufactures industrial & specialty chemicals for lubricant, oil & gas, and water treatment industries. Currently, many target customers buy the same chemicals from different vendors under different brand names. This website positions VasuDev as a direct, trusted, export-ready manufacturer — eliminating middlemen and building global brand recognition.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Direct manufacturer** — not a trader or reseller
- **ISO 9001:2015 certified** quality
- **Gujarat location** — India's largest chemical manufacturing hub, near major ports (Kandla, Mundra, Hazira)
- **15+ chemical products** with export-ready packaging
- **Competitive pricing** with reliable global shipping
- **8+ years** of manufacturing experience

## Primary Goal
**Generate global export/import inquiries.** Every section of the website funnels visitors toward the inquiry form or WhatsApp contact. Target markets: Gulf countries, USA & Canada, Middle East & Africa, Southeast Asia, Europe.

## Company Details
- **Name**: VasuDev Chemicals (Vasudev Chemo Pharma)
- **Industry**: Industrial & Specialty Chemical Manufacturing
- **Location**: Gujarat, India
- **Experience**: 8+ years
- **Products**: 15+ chemicals (sodium sulphate, zinc oxide, titanium dioxide, calcium carbonate, caustic soda, etc.)
- **Certifications**: ISO 9001:2015
- **Services**: Manufacturing, import/export of chemicals
